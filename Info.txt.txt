Aliens Blew Up My Cubicle 

Made by: Austin Winkler, Torrey Wang, Alyssa Mazzucotelli, and Samantha Gomes 

Aliens Blew Up My Cubicle is a text-based adventure where you play as a office worker named Jake. 
His best friend is a cactus named Jeff, and aliens have kidnapped him, so you must go on a journey with choices and dialouge to save him. 

Austin Winkler: Main Designer, Main Story Writer, Art Designer 
Torrey Wang: Story Writer, Coder 
Alyssa Mazzucotelli: Coder, Researcher 
Samantha Gomes: Story Writer, Coder, Editer
